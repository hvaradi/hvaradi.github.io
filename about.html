Who is JAX?

After several years of working together in the restaurant industry, business partners Woody Fox and Ed Manns decided to apply for a Golden Corral franchise in 1996. By 1997, they opened their first store in Statesville, NC. In December of 1999, they opened their second Golden Corral, and have never looked back.

Today, JAX LLC is currently operating twelve Golden Corral restaurants in North Carolina, South Carolina, and Pennsylvania, and still continues to grow.

Throughout the years, various employees have joined our team to assist in the overall operation, including Bethany Bryan, the Executive Business Manager, and Kathy Ortiz, the AP Manager.
